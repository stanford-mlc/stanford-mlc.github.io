Our lab focuses on developing novel machine learning algorithms for next-generation wireless systems, communications modulation and coding, signal processing and communication theory.

Director: John M. Cioffi
Hitachi Professor Emeritus of Engineering [Recalled]

Research Areas

Dynamic spectrum management Communications modulation & coding Signal processing Wireless networks 5G/6G Systems Digital transmission Resource optimization

Lab Leadership & Recognition

Our lab is led by Prof. John M. Cioffi, recipient of the 2023 National Technology and Innovation Medal from President Biden, Marconi Fellow (2006), Member of the National Academy of Engineering (2001), and holder of numerous IEEE awards including the Alexander Graham Bell Medal (2010). Prof. Cioffi is widely recognized as the "Father of DSL" and a pioneer in digital communications.
